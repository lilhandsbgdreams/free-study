About

Today's technology allows us to take learning into our own hands. Here at Free Study, we seek to help you do this by connecting your interest in learning a subject with the free means of learning that subject on the Internet. Where there's a will to learn, there's a way!

Through the Internet, we now have access to both a huge amount of information about many subjects and to networks of people who share similar learning goals. Free Study connects you to those learning resources through easy to browse categories. You simply visit our site, find a subject, and start learning.

Free Study was created with the belief that one's learning ambitions should not be limited. For everyone out there that has an interest in many different subjects, but feels like they don't have the time or means to pursue all of those learning goals, we're here for you. If you have the will to learn it, we can connect you with the means to do so, because your intelligence should only be driven by you.
